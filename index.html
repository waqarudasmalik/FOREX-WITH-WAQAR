Hedging Kya Hai?

Hedging aik risk-management strategy hai jismein trader ek position ke against dusri opposite position khol kar apne losses ko limit karta hai.

Basic Hedging Rule

Agar aapki BUY position loss mein ja rahi hai, to same pair par ek SELL position khol dein taake dono positions ka net loss control mein rahe.
