Hedging System

Hedging Kya Hai?

Hedging aik risk-management strategy hai jismein trader ek position ke against dusri opposite position khol kar apne losses ko limit karta hai.

Basic Hedging Rule

Agar aapki BUY position loss mein ja rahi hai, to same pair par ek SELL position khol dein taake dono positions ka net loss control mein rahe.

Lot Size Balance

Hedge karte waqt lot size ka hisaab rakhein — agar original position 0.10 lot ki hai to hedge bhi usi ratio mein hona chahiye.

Exit Plan

Hedge sirf temporary protection ke liye hoti hai. Market direction clear hote hi loss wali side ko close karke sirf profitable side ko continue rakhein.
